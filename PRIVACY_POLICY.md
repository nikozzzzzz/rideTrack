# Privacy Policy for FreeRide Tracker

**Last Updated**: November 29, 2025

## Introduction

FreeRide Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect**: GPS coordinates, altitude, speed, and timestamp during activity tracking
- **How we use it**: To track your rides, calculate statistics, and display routes on maps
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device and optionally in your private iCloud account
- **Sharing**: We do NOT share, sell, or transmit your location data to third parties

### User Profile Information (Optional)
- **What we collect**: Name, profile picture, and activity preferences (if you choose to provide them)
- **How we use it**: To personalize your experience and sync data across your devices
- **Storage**: Stored in your private iCloud account
- **Sharing**: We do NOT share your profile information with third parties

### Activity Data
- **What we collect**: Ride statistics (distance, duration, speed, activity type)
- **How we use it**: To provide activity history, analytics, and performance tracking
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device and optionally in your private iCloud account
- **Sharing**: We do NOT share your activity data with third parties

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:
1. **App Functionality**: Tracking your activities and providing statistics
2. **Data Synchronization**: Syncing your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
3. **App Improvement**: Understanding app usage to improve features (anonymized only)

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- **iCloud Sync**: If enabled, data is synchronized to your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit
- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using Apple's security standards
- **Access**: Only you have access to your data through your Apple ID

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### Access and Control
- **View Your Data**: All your data is accessible within the app
- **Export Your Data**: Use the app's export feature to download your data
- **Delete Your Data**: Delete individual rides or all data from the app settings
- **Disable iCloud Sync**: Turn off iCloud synchronization at any time

### Location Permissions
- **Control Access**: Manage location permissions in iOS Settings ‚Üí FreeRide Tracker
- **Background Location**: Required for continuous tracking; can be disabled
- **Revoke Permission**: You can revoke location access at any time

### Data Deletion
To delete all your data:
1. Open the app settings
2. Select "Delete All Data"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Optionally, delete the app to remove all local data

- **Active Data**: Retained as long as you use the app
- **Deleted Data**: Permanently deleted from your device and iCloud when you delete it
